vibelords: melee strike-accent VFX for all 8 pike/cavalry units. Each body's attack motion gains a signature accent phase-locked to its weapon keyframe (same animation duration, flashing in the window where the strike lands): Clubman swoosh crescent + impact burst, Pikeman thrust flash at the leveled tip, Trench Guard & Dragoon muzzle flash + smoke, Juggernaut energy discharge off the lance orb, Wolf Rider & Knight tip burst + speed lines, Hover Striker bright beam core + ground flare. Pure renderer change — no engine fx involved, so the accents appear on existing replays too. Asset-sheet unit cells get a wider/taller viewBox so right-facing attack accents aren't clipped.
